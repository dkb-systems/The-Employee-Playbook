Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.

Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is always available with no time limits. For paid plans, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay for monthly subscriptions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No questions asked, no hidden fees.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and never share your personal data. You can read our full privacy policy for more details.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes! Save 20% with annual billing. Choose annual billing during checkout to get two months free compared to monthly billing.
